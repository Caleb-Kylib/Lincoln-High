Explore Our School Clubs

Debate Club

Our Debate Club sharpens students’ critical thinking and public speaking. Members engage in local and national debates, building confidence and communication skills.

Journalism Club

The Journalism Club empowers students to write creatively and report school events. They produce articles, interviews, and newsletters that tell our school’s story.

Arts Club

Students express themselves through painting, drawing, and crafts. The Arts Club showcases talents during school exhibitions and cultural days.

Chess Club

The Chess Club trains strategic thinking and patience. Open to all levels, members participate in friendly matches and inter-school tournaments.

Drama Club

The Drama Club brings stories to life through acting, scripting, and stage production. Performances are held during school events and festivals.

Science Club

Curious minds experiment and innovate in the Science Club. Members engage in STEM projects, science fairs, and discovery-based learning activities.
