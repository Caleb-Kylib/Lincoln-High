Sports We Offer

Football

Our football program is competitive and inclusive, giving students an opportunity to train, compete, and grow under experienced coaches.

Athletics

From sprints to long-distance, our athletics team is active in local meets. We focus on strength, endurance, and discipline.

Volleyball

Our boys and girls volleyball teams train weekly and regularly participate in school tournaments and friendlies.

Basketball

With a modern court and vibrant student teams, our basketball program promotes teamwork, speed, and strategic thinking.

Swimming

Our swimming pool is open to all students for training and competition. We train swimmers of all levels.

Rugby

Known for discipline and power, our rugby team competes in regional tournaments and trains throughout the year.

Netball

Our netball girls team is among the most disciplined and competitive in the district, with regular training and match play.
